## Agile Ceremonies Conducted

### Daily Standups
- **Frequency:** Every weekday (Mon-Fri)
- **Duration:** 15 minutes
- **Format:** What did you do? What will you do? Any blockers?

### Sprint Planning
- **Frequency:** Start of each sprint
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Outcome:** Sprint backlog with story points

### Sprint Review
- **Frequency:** End of each sprint
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Attendees:** Team + Mentor + Stakeholders

### Sprint Retrospective
- **Frequency:** End of each sprint
- **Duration:** 45 minutes
- **Format:** Start-Stop-Continue + Action Items
